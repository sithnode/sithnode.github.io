About

The Bridge Between Business and Systems

I started at Investor's Business Daily learning that data only matters when it reaches the right person in the right format. That principle guides every system I build.

From there I moved into hedge fund operations at O'Neil Global Advisors, leading GIPS compliance certification, administering portfolio management systems, and designing data flows connecting performance metrics and investor reporting across SQL Server and PostgreSQL.

Today at Viking River Cruises I lead Apple Pay integration, migrate financial reporting from SSRS to Power BI, build Databricks pipelines, and bridge third-party bank processors with internal development teams.

Outside my day role, I am building AI agent systems, local LLM infrastructure, and knowledge architectures for the next era of enterprise operations.
